Homework Assignment Guidelines

  • Homework is submitted through our submissions page. LaTeX submissions are encouraged. We recommend overleaf for everyone who is new to LaTeX.
  • Homework will be double blind peer reviewed as indicated in the syllabus.
  • Indicate your answers clearly and neatly when appropriate. You need not box in answers (although occasionally this helps), but you do need to make clear what your final answer is when appropriate.
    •
  • Use good mathematical style when writing proofs (more will be said about this in class). In particular, avoid tedious run-on sentences. Concision and elegance count.
  • See the page of common mistakes.
